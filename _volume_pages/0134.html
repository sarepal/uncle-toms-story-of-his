{% raw %}The next season I brought a large cargo by the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}River St. Lawrence, which came direct to Boston,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}where, without the aid of any agent or third party{% endraw %}

{% raw %}some profit. A little incident occurred when paying-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the duties, which has often since afforded me a great{% endraw %}
{% raw %}deal of amusement.. The Fugitive Slave Law had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}just been passed in the United States, which made{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it quite an offence to harbour or render aid to a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fugitive slave. When the Custom House officer pre¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sented his bill to me for the duties on my lumber, I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}jokingly remarked to him that perhaps he would.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}render himself liable to trouble if he should have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dealings with a fugitive slave, and if so, I would,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}relieve him of the trouble of taking my money.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}" Are you a fugitive slave, sir ? " " Yes, sir," said{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I; " and perhaps you had better not have any{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dealings with me." "I have nothing to do with{% endraw %}
