{% raw %}—SAFE AT HOME.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The degraded and hopeless condition of a slave{% endraw %}
{% raw %}can never be properly felt by him while he{% endraw %}
{% raw %}remains in such a position. After I had tasted the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}blessings of freedom, my mind reverted to those{% endraw %}
{% raw %}whom I knew were groaning in captivity, and I at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}once proceeded to take measures to free as many as{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I could. I thought that, by using exertion, num¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bers might make their escape as I did, if they had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}some practical advice how to proceed.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Erie, at which a great many coloured people were{% endraw %}
{% raw %}present. In the course of my preaching, I tried to{% endraw %}

{% raw %}tions they were under ; first, to God, for their de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}liverance ; and then, secondly, to their fellow-men,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to do all that was in their power to bring others out{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of bondage. In the congregation was a man named{% endraw %}
{% raw %}James Lightfoot, who was of a very active tempera¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Canada, but had never thought of his family and{% endraw %}
