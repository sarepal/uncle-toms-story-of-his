{% raw %}Maryland, on a farm belonging to Mr. Francis{% endraw %}

{% raw %}man's farm, was the appearance one day of my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}father with his head bloody and his back lacerated.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}He was beside himself with mingled rage and suffer¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing. The overseer had brutally assaulted my mother,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}when my father sprang upon him like a tiger. In{% endraw %}

{% raw %}rage, my father would have killed him but for the{% endraw %}

{% raw %}matter. The promise was kept—like most promises{% endraw %}
