{% raw %}were disposed to make the attempt. At length, on a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Saturday night, we started. The agony of parting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}can be better conceived than described ; as, in their{% endraw %}
{% raw %}oase, husbands were leaving their wives, mothers{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their children, and children their parents. This, at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}first sight, will appear strange, and even incredible ;{% endraw %}
{% raw %}but, when we take into consideration the fact, that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}at any time they were liable to be separated, by{% endraw %}
{% raw %}being sold to what are termed "nigger traders,"{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and the probability that such an event would take{% endraw %}
{% raw %}place, it will, I think, cease to excite any surprise.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}We succeeded in crossing the Ohio River in safety,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and arrived in Cincinnati the third night after our{% endraw %}
{% raw %}departure. Here we procured assistance; and, after{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stopping a short time to rest, we started for Rich¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mond, Indiana. This is a town which had been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}settled by Quakers, and there we found friends in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}deed, who at once helped us on our way, without{% endraw %}
{% raw %}loss of time ; and after a difficult journey of two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}weeks, through the wilderness, we reached Toledo,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Ohio, a town on the south-western shore of Lake{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Erie, and there we took passage for Canada, which{% endraw %}
{% raw %}we reached in safety. I then went home to my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}family, taking with me a part of this large party,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the rest finding their friends scattered in other{% endraw %}
{% raw %}towns, perfectly satisfied with my conduct in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}matter, in being permitted to be the instrument of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}freeing such a number of my fellow-creatures.{% endraw %}
