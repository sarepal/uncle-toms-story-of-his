{% raw %}run away, Sie, to my brother Amos in Kentucky,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and take all the servants along with you." I could{% endraw %}

{% raw %}don't know the way." " Oh, it's easy enough for a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}smart fellow like you to find it; I'll give you a pass{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and tell you just what to do." Perceiving that I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hesitated, he endeavoured to frighten me by again{% endraw %}
{% raw %}referring to the terrors of being sold and taken to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Georgia.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}For two or three hours he continued to urge the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}undertaking, appealing to my pride, my sympathies,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and my fears, and at last, appalling as it seemed, I{% endraw %}

{% raw %}negroes, besides my wife, two children, and myself{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to transport nearly a thousand miles, through, a{% endraw %}
