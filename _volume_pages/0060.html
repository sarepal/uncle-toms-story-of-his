{% raw %}EETTJEN TO MAEYLAXD.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}RECEPTION FROM MY OLD MASTER.—A SLAVE AGAIN.—APPEAL TO{% endraw %}
{% raw %}AN OLD FRIEND.—BUY' MY FREEDOM.—CHEATED AND BETRAYED.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}--BACK TO KENTUCKY, AND A SLAVE AGAIN.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Before I left Ohio and set my face towards Mont¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}gomery county, I was master of two hundred{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and seventy-five dollars, besides my horse and clothes.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Proud of my success, I enjoyed the thought of show¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing myself once more in the place where I had been{% endraw %}
{% raw %}known simply as " Riley's head-nigger; " and it was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with no little satisfaction that about Christmas I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rode up to the old house.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}My master gave me a boisterous reception, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}expressed great delight at seeing me. " What have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you been doing, Sie ? you've turned into a regular{% endraw %}
{% raw %}black gentleman." My horse and dress sorely{% endraw %}
{% raw %}puzzled him, and I soon saw they irritated him.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The clothes I wore were certainly better than his.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Very soon the workings of that tyrannical hate with{% endraw %}
{% raw %}which the coarse and brutal, who have no inherent{% endraw %}
{% raw %}superiority, ever regard the least sign of equality in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}their dependents, were visible in his manner. His{% endraw %}
{% raw %}face seemed to say, " I'll take the gentleman out of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}you pretty soon." I gave him an account of my{% endraw %}
