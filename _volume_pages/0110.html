{% raw %}106 MRS. H. BEECHER STOWE's " UNCLE TOM."{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I discovered, before long, that McCormick had{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not complied with the conditions of his grant, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was not, therefore, entitled to the rent he exacted{% endraw %}
{% raw %}from the settlers. I was advised by Sir John Cock-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}burn, to whom I applied on the subject, to appeal to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the legislature for relief. We did so ; and though{% endraw %}
{% raw %}McCormick was able, by the aid of his friends, to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}defeat us for one year, yet we succeeded the next,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}upon a second appeal, and were freed from all rent{% endraw %}
{% raw %}thereafter, so long as we remained. Still, this was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not our own land. The government, though it de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}manded no rent, might set up the land for sale at{% endraw %}
{% raw %}any time, and then we should, probably, be driven{% endraw %}
{% raw %}off by wealthier purchasers, with the entire loss of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}all our improvements, and with no retreat provided.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}It was manifest that it was altogether better for us{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to purchase before competition was invited; and we{% endraw %}
{% raw %}kept this fully in mind during the time we stayed{% endraw %}
{% raw %}there. We remained in this position six or seven{% endraw %}
{% raw %}years; and all this while the coloured population{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was increasing rapidly around us, and spreading{% endraw %}
{% raw %}very fast into the interior settlements and the large{% endraw %}
{% raw %}towns. The immigration from the United States{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was incessant, and some, I am willing to admit,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}were brought hither with my knowledge and con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}nivance ; and I will now proceed to give a short{% endraw %}
{% raw %}account of the plans and operations I had arranged{% endraw %}
{% raw %}for the liberation of some of my brethren, which I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hope may prove interesting to the reader.{% endraw %}
