{% raw %}dear wife, the mother of my children, she who had{% endraw %}

{% raw %}affectionate, and dutiful wife for forty years, was{% endraw %}

{% raw %}A merciful Father had, however, kindly prolonged{% endraw %}
{% raw %}her life, and we were permitted once more to meet.{% endraw %}
