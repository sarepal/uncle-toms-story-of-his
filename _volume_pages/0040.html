{% raw %}The moment he saw me, he hallooed, "That's it,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sie ! pitch in ! show me fair play." It was a rough{% endraw %}
{% raw %}business, and I went in roughly, shoving, tripping,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and doing my best for the rescue. With infinite{% endraw %}
{% raw %}trouble, and many a bruise on my own head and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shoulders, I at length got him out of the room. He{% endraw %}
{% raw %}was crazy with drink and rage, and struggled hard{% endraw %}
{% raw %}with me to get back and renew the fight. But I{% endraw %}
{% raw %}managed to force him into his waggon, jump in,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and drive off.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}By ill-luck, in the height of the scuffle, Bryce{% endraw %}
