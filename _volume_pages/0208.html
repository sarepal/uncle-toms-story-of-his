{% raw %}After the lapse of twenty-five years I was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}delighted to be in London again. Many of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the friends whom I had known during my former{% endraw %}
{% raw %}visit have passed away. I found that Samuel Morlej^,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Esq., and George Sturge, Esq., remembered me{% endraw %}

{% raw %}pressing down my spirits and embarrassing my de¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}clining years when I could not labour as formerly.{% endraw %}
