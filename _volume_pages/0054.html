{% raw %}privileges, which, since I first heard of Christ and{% endraw %}

{% raw %}Kentucky the opportunities of attending the preach¬{% endraw %}

{% raw %}me, in all the stations of life which I could watch, I{% endraw %}

{% raw %}best to arouse them, and keep them excited, how to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}stir up the callous and indifferent, and, in general,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to produce some good religious impressions on the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ignorant and thoughtless community by which I was{% endraw %}
