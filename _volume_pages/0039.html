{% raw %}he could not do for himself, showing my superiority{% endraw %}

{% raw %}On one of these occasions my master got into a{% endraw %}
{% raw %}quarrel with his brother's overseer, Bryce Litton.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}All present sided with Litton against him, and soon{% endraw %}
{% raw %}there was a general row. I was sitting, at the time,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}out on the front steps of the tavern, and, hearing-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the scuffle, rushed in to look after my charge. My{% endraw %}
{% raw %}master, a stout man and a terrible bruiser, could{% endraw %}
