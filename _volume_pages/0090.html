{% raw %}CANADA.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I now felt comparatively at home. Before entering{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the town I hid my wife and children in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}woods, and then walked on alone in search of my{% endraw %}
{% raw %}friends. They welcomed me warmly, and just after{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dusk my wife and children were brought in, and we{% endraw %}
{% raw %}found ourselves hospitably cheered and refreshed.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Two weeks of exposure to incessant fatigue, anxiety,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}rain, and chill, made it indescribably sweet to enjoy{% endraw %}
{% raw %}once more the comfort of rest and shelter.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I have sometimes heard harsh and bitter words{% endraw %}
{% raw %}spoken of those devoted men who were banded{% endraw %}
{% raw %}together to succour and bid God speed to the hunted{% endraw %}
{% raw %}fugitive; men who, through pity for the suffering,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}voluntarily exposed themselves to hatred, fines, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}imprisonment. If there be a God who will have{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mercy on the merciful, great will be their reward.{% endraw %}
