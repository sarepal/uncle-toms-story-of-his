{% raw %}SONGS.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sharp flashes of lightning come from black clouds,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}sprightly words of wit come from those who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}live in dark hovels, and bright gleams of intelligence{% endraw %}
{% raw %}come from children brought up in the most abject{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ignorance of books. It has often been a mystery to{% endraw %}
{% raw %}me how I gained a practical knowledge of figures,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}enough to sell all the produce of four farms during{% endraw %}
{% raw %}twenty-five years in the market at Washington, for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I had'to compute fractions and make great estimates,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}and yet I never studied arithmetic. I came in con¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tact with many of the most intelligent gentlemen in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Washington, "for I used to take great pride in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}selecting the best butter for some of the best families,{% endraw %}

{% raw %}conversed I listened attentively and remembered{% endraw %}

{% raw %}slaves, or even the poor whites of the district. I{% endraw %}
