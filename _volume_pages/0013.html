{% raw %}In response to our request for a few words intro¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ductory to " Uncle Tom's Life," we have the follow¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}ing from George Sturge. "My knowledge of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Josiah He{% endraw %}{% raw %}nson dates from his visit to this country{% endraw %}
{% raw %}twenty-five years ago, when my late brother Thomas{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sturge, with other friends of the negro race, helped{% endraw %}
{% raw %}to establish ' The Dawn Institute for the Education{% endraw %}
{% raw %}of Coloured People in Canada.' I regard Josiah{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Henson in many respects as a remarkable man.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}When I contemplate his unselfish efforts (at great{% endraw %}
{% raw %}risk to himself) to re{% endraw %}{% raw %}scue his brethren in slavery,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}after he had obtained his own liberty, and his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}labours as a free man to educate and enlighten them,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I consider that there are few men now living: who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}have done so much for the negro race. When{% endraw %}
{% raw %}it is remembered, too, that he was a slave for{% endraw %}
{% raw %}forty-two years, his life affords an encouraging{% endraw %}
{% raw %}example of what may be done, even by one who has{% endraw %}
{% raw %}laboured under the greatest disadvantages, who{% endraw %}
{% raw %}is earnestly desirous to benefit his race. His{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Christian simplicity, and the absence of all bitter{% endraw %}
{% raw %}feeling towards those who have oppressed him, will{% endraw %}
{% raw %}have commended him to all who have made his{% endraw %}
{% raw %}acquaintance. The life of 'Uncle Tom,' now ex¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}tended in its records to the present date, will be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}found by its readers to possess deep interest, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}will doubtless be favourably received. On submitting{% endraw %}
{% raw %}these observations to Samuel Morley, his remark{% endraw %}
{% raw %}"Was, ' I THOROUGHLY AGREE WITH THEM.' "{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Sydenham, Oct. 1876.{% endraw %}
