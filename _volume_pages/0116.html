{% raw %}I had a very narrow escape from being detected.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The place was frequented by a number of Kentuckians,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}who were quite ready to suspect a coloured man, if{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they saw anything unusual about him. I reached{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Portsmouth in the morning, and waited until two{% endraw %}
{% raw %}in the afternoon for the steamboat, so that I might{% endraw %}
{% raw %}not arrive in Maysville till after dark. While in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the town I was obliged to resort to a stratagem, in{% endraw %}
{% raw %}order to avoid being questioned by the Kentuckians-{% endraw %}
{% raw %}I saw in the place. To this end I procured some{% endraw %}
{% raw %}dried leaves, put them into a cloth and bound it all{% endraw %}
{% raw %}round my face, reaching nearly to my eyes, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pretended to be so seriously affected in my head and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}teeth as not to be able to speak. I then hung{% endraw %}
{% raw %}around the village till the time for the evening boat,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}so as to arrive at Maysville in the night. I was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}accosted by several during my short stay in Ports¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mouth, who appeared very anxious to get some{% endraw %}
{% raw %}particulars from me as to who I was, where I was{% endraw %}
{% raw %}going, and to whom I belonged. To all their{% endraw %}
{% raw %}numerous inquiries I merely shook my head,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}mumbled out indistinct answers, and acted so that{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they could not get anything out of me ; and, by this{% endraw %}
{% raw %}artifice, I succeeded in avoiding any unpleasant{% endraw %}
{% raw %}consequences. I got on board the boat and reached{% endraw %}
