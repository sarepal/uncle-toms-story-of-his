{% raw %}PREFACE.{% endraw %}
{% raw %}The African race appear as yet to have been com¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}panions onlv of the sufferings of Christ. In the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}melancholy scene of His death—while Europe in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}person of the Roman delivered Him unto death, and{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Asia in the person of the Jew clamoured for His{% endraw %}
{% raw %}execution—Africa was represented in the person of{% endraw %}
{% raw %}Simon the Cyrenean, who came patiently bearing{% endraw %}
{% raw %}after Him. the load of the cross; and ever since then{% endraw %}
{% raw %}poor Africa has been toiling on, bearing the weary{% endraw %}
{% raw %}cross of contempt and oppression after Jesus. But{% endraw %}
{% raw %}they who suffer with Him shall also reign; and when{% endraw %}
{% raw %}the unwritten annals of slavery shall appear in the{% endraw %}
{% raw %}judgment, many Simons who have gone meekly{% endraw %}
{% raw %}bearing their cross after Jesus to unknown graves,{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shall rise to thrones and crowns ! Verily a day{% endraw %}
{% raw %}shall come when He shall appear for these His{% endraw %}
{% raw %}hidden ones, and then " many that are last shall be{% endraw %}
{% raw %}first, and the first shall be last."{% endraw %}
