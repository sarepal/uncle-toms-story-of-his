{% raw %}I urged the appropriation of the money to the{% endraw %}

{% raw %}that it would gradually enable us to become inde¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}pendent of the white man for our intellectual{% endraw %}
{% raw %}progress, as we could be for our physical prosperity.{% endraw %}

{% raw %}owing to the insurmountable prejudices of the in¬{% endraw %}
{% raw %}habitants, the children of the blacks were not allowed{% endraw %}
